Zero Waste is an organization that provides daily training for sustainability life. It has hundreds of participants learning online. This project was used as a gift for participants. The project required outcomes that match the brand cultures and does something small for sustainability daily life.

The self-checking tote bag is a product for daily use for Zero Waste users. Avoiding using plastic bags is the first step in sustainability in everyday life. Recording cycle patterns helps users track their daily learning and practicing by checking with a scrubbable marker.
